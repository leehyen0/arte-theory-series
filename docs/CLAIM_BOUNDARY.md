# Claim Boundary

This repository is an index for a public theory-first series.

It does **not** claim:

- AGI,
- superintelligence,
- consciousness,
- sentience,
- paranormal precognition,
- literal quantum cognition,
- literal quantum consciousness,
- model-weight modification,
- public/offbox proof,
- official benchmark validation,
- validated experimental results,
- externally reproduced performance improvement,
- safety certification,
- deployment readiness,
- that generated candidate laws are true laws merely because they were generated,
- that questions physically alter reality.

## Terms that need careful framing

### "Self-transcendent"

In this series, "self-transcendent" means computational present-policy revision by a wider temporal self-model. It does not mean metaphysical transcendence.

### "Pre-cognition" or "future-state metacognition"

This means internal modeling and evaluation of possible future states. It does not mean paranormal precognition.

### "Imaginary" or "complementary"

These terms refer to computationally preserved unrealized alternatives or auxiliary representational axes. They do not imply literal quantum cognition or quantum consciousness.

### "QuestionField"

This means a structured inquiry field over belief states and preserved alternatives. It is not a literal physical field.

### "LawGenesis"

This means a procedure for forming, testing, rejecting, or consuming candidate patterns. It does not mean generated patterns are automatically true laws.

## Evidence rule

Internal logs, local traces, prompt histories, controller seeds, worker results, or private runtime artifacts are development context only.

They must not be presented as public proof, external reproduction, official benchmark validation, model-weight modification evidence, or deployment-ready safety evidence.
